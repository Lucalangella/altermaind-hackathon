ARTICLE 1 - SUBJECT MATTER AND SCOPE
================================================================================

1.1 DataVista Cloud Platform - Enterprise License
    - Up to 500 concurrent users
    - Unlimited data storage
    - Annual license fee: EUR 320,000.00

1.2 Data Analytics Module - Advanced Tier
    - Real-time analytics dashboards
    - Predictive maintenance integration
    - Annual fee: EUR 85,000.00

1.3 Implementation and Integration Services
    - ERP system integration (SAP S/4HANA)
    - Data migration from legacy systems
    - One-time fee: EUR 45,000.00

1.4 Training Program
    - 5-day onsite training for up to 30 users
    - Online training portal access for 12 months
    - Fee: EUR 12,000.00

Total Annual Contract Value: EUR 417,000.00
Total Contract Value (36 months): EUR 1,296,000.00

================================================================================
ARTICLE 2 - TERM AND RENEWAL
================================================================================

2.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue
for a period of thirty-six (36) months, expiring on January 14, 2027.

2.2 The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of
twelve (12) months each, unless either party provides written notice of
non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the expiration of the
then-current term.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 3 - PRICING AND PAYMENT
================================================================================

3.1 Payment shall be made quarterly in advance, within thirty (30) days
of invoice date.

3.2 Annual price adjustments shall not exceed the ISTAT consumer price
index variation plus 2%.

3.3 A volume discount of 8% is applied to the Cloud Platform license,
reflecting the multi-year commitment. This discount is standard per the
Provider's published volume discount schedule.

3.4 Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 3.5% per annum above
the ECB reference rate.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 4 - SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
================================================================================

4.1 Platform Availability: The Provider guarantees a monthly uptime of
99.5% for the Cloud Platform, measured on a calendar month basis.

4.2 Support Response Times:
    - Critical Issues (P1): Response within 2 hours, resolution target 8 hours
    - Major Issues (P2): Response within 4 hours, resolution target 24 hours
    - Minor Issues (P3): Response within 8 hours, resolution target 72 hours

4.3 SLA Credits: For each 0.1% below the guaranteed uptime, the Client
shall receive a service credit equal to 2% of the monthly platform fee,
up to a maximum of 15% of the monthly fee.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 5 - PENALTIES
================================================================================

5.1 Data Migration Delay: Should the Provider fail to complete the data
migration within the agreed timeline of 90 days from contract signing,
a penalty of EUR 1,500 per week of delay shall apply, capped at EUR 15,000.

5.2 SLA Breach: Repeated failure to meet SLA targets (defined as three
consecutive months below guaranteed uptime) shall entitle the Client to
terminate the Agreement with 30 days' notice and receive a refund of
prepaid fees on a pro-rata basis.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 6 - LIABILITY
================================================================================

6.1 The Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not
exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the twelve (12) months
preceding the claim.

6.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or
consequential damages.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 7 - TERMINATION
================================================================================

7.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause upon sixty (60)
days' written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement
and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days of receiving notice.

7.2 Termination for convenience is permitted after the initial 24-month
period, subject to a 90-day notice period and payment of an early
termination fee equal to 25% of the remaining contract value.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 8 - GOVERNING LAW
================================================================================

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the
laws of Italy. Any disputes shall be submitted to the exclusive
jurisdiction of the Court of Milan.

2. DURATION
================================================================================

2.1 This Agreement is effective from March 1, 2024 and shall remain in
force for forty-eight (48) months, terminating on February 28, 2028.

2.2 Automatic renewal applies for successive 24-month periods unless
either party gives at least one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written
notice before expiration.

================================================================================
3. FINANCIAL TERMS
================================================================================

3.1 Invoicing shall occur monthly in arrears.

3.2 Payment is due within forty-five (45) days of invoice date.

3.3 A strategic partnership discount of 22% has been applied to the
standard list price of the Cloud Platform. This discount was negotiated as
part of a custom arrangement and exceeds the standard volume discount
schedule (maximum 15%).

   NOTE: Standard list price for Enterprise Plus is EUR 743,590/year.
   Applied discount brings this to EUR 580,000/year.

3.4 Late payment interest: 5% per annum.

3.5 Payment shall be made in EUR to the Provider's designated bank account.

================================================================================
4. SERVICE LEVELS
================================================================================

4.1 Platform Uptime: 99.99% monthly availability guarantee.
    (NOTE: Standard offering is 99.5%. This is a custom SLA negotiated
    specifically for this client.)

4.2 Response Times:
    - P1 Critical: 15-minute response, 2-hour resolution target
    - P2 Major: 1-hour response, 8-hour resolution target
    - P3 Minor: 4-hour response, 48-hour resolution target

4.3 For each 0.01% below the 99.99% uptime guarantee, the Client is
entitled to a service credit of 5% of the monthly platform fee. Credits
are uncapped.

================================================================================
5. PENALTIES AND CONSEQUENCES
================================================================================

5.1 Chronic SLA Failure: If the platform availability falls below 99.9%
in any single month, the Client may demand a remediation plan within 5
business days. If the Provider fails to deliver the plan, the Client may
terminate immediately.

5.2 Data Breach Penalty: In the event of a data breach attributable to
Provider negligence, the Provider shall pay liquidated damages of
EUR 500,000 per incident, in addition to any actual damages.

5.3 Delivery Delays: For any milestone delivered more than 15 business
days late, a penalty of 2% of the milestone value per week of delay
applies, with no cap.

================================================================================
6. LIABILITY
================================================================================

6.1 IMPORTANT: The Provider accepts UNLIMITED LIABILITY for:
    - Data breaches caused by Provider negligence
    - Intellectual property infringement
    - Willful misconduct

6.2 For all other claims, liability is capped at 200% of the annual
contract value.

6.3 The Provider shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client against
any third-party claims arising from the Provider's breach of data
protection obligations under GDPR.

7.2 The Client retains exclusive rights to any derivative insights

8.3 No early termination fee applies if termination is due to Provider's
SLA failures as defined in Section 5.1.

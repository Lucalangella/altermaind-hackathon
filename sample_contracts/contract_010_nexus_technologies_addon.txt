================================================================================
                AMENDMENT AND ADDENDUM NO. 1
            to Master Service Agreement CTR-2024-002
                    Ref: CTR-2024-002-A1
================================================================================

Date: December 1, 2024

This Amendment modifies the Master Service Agreement CTR-2024-002
dated March 1, 2024 between DataVista Solutions S.r.l. ("Provider") and
Nexus Technologies GmbH ("Client").

================================================================================
1. PURPOSE
================================================================================

The Client wishes to expand the scope of services under the original
Agreement to include additional AI/ML capabilities and increase the
platform capacity.

================================================================================
2. ADDITIONAL SERVICES
================================================================================

The following services are added to the Agreement:

2.1 Generative AI Document Processing Module
    - Contract analysis and extraction
    - Automated compliance checking
    - Multi-language support (DE, EN, IT, FR)
    - Annual fee: EUR 180,000.00

2.2 Advanced Computer Vision Module
    - Quality inspection automation
    - Defect detection for manufacturing clients
    - Annual fee: EUR 95,000.00

2.3 Platform Capacity Upgrade
    - Additional region: EU-North (Stockholm)
    - Increased compute allocation: +50%
    - Additional annual fee: EUR 120,000.00

Additional Annual Value: EUR 395,000.00
Revised Total Annual Contract Value: EUR 1,470,000.00

================================================================================
3. REVISED FINANCIAL TERMS
================================================================================

3.1 The strategic partnership discount is increased from 22% to 25%
on the Cloud Platform component, effective from the date of this Amendment.

    *** FURTHER DISCOUNT INCREASE: Now 25%, well above the 15% standard
    maximum. Justified by the expanded scope and total contract value. ***

3.2 A new volume commitment tier is established:
    - If annual spend exceeds EUR 1,500,000: additional 3% rebate
    - If annual spend exceeds EUR 2,000,000: additional 5% rebate

3.3 All other financial terms from CTR-2024-002 remain unchanged.

================================================================================
4. REVISED SLA FOR NEW MODULES
================================================================================

4.1 The Generative AI module shall have a separate SLA:
    - Accuracy guarantee: 95% extraction accuracy on structured fields
    - Processing time: 95th percentile < 30 seconds per document
    - Availability: 99.9%

4.2 Computer Vision module:
    - Accuracy guarantee: 98% defect detection rate
    - False positive rate: < 2%
    - Availability: 99.9%

================================================================================
5. ADDITIONAL PENALTY PROVISIONS
================================================================================

5.1 AI Accuracy Failure: If the Generative AI module fails to maintain
95% accuracy for 3 consecutive months, the Client may:
    a) Demand a remediation plan within 10 business days
    b) Withhold payment for the affected module until accuracy is restored
    c) Terminate the module subscription with 30 days' notice

5.2 If the Computer Vision module produces false positives exceeding 5%
in a production environment, resulting in Client's customer complaints,
the Provider shall be liable for actual damages up to EUR 500,000.

================================================================================
6. TERM
================================================================================

6.1 This Amendment is effective from December 1, 2024 and shall run
co-terminus with the original Agreement (through February 28, 2028).

================================================================================
7. ALL OTHER TERMS
================================================================================

All other terms and conditions of CTR-2024-002 remain in full force
and effect. In case of conflict between this Amendment and the original
Agreement, this Amendment shall prevail.

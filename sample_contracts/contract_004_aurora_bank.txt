================================================================================

    - Up to 1,000 users across 85 branches
    - Annual license: EUR 480,000.00

    - Transaction pattern detection
    - Suspicious activity reporting automation
    - Annual fee: EUR 165,000.00

1.3 Risk Analytics Dashboard
    - Credit risk modeling
    - Market risk visualization
    - Operational risk tracking
    - Annual fee: EUR 120,000.00

1.4 Regulatory Reporting Automation
    - COREP/FINREP automated generation
    - ECB statistical reporting
    - Annual fee: EUR 85,000.00

1.5 Annual Maintenance and Support
    - Annual fee: EUR 50,000.00

Total Annual Contract Value: EUR 900,000.00
Total Contract Value (60 months): EUR 4,500,000.00

================================================================================
SECTION 2 - TERM
================================================================================

2.1 The Agreement commences on September 1, 2023 and shall continue for
a period of sixty (60) months, expiring on August 31, 2028.

2.2 Automatic renewal for successive 12-month periods applies unless
either party provides 120 days' written notice of non-renewal.

================================================================================
SECTION 3 - PRICING AND PAYMENT
================================================================================

3.1 Payment: Quarterly in advance, net 30 days.

3.2 A loyalty discount of 12% has been applied, reflecting the Bank's
status as a long-standing client since 2019.

3.3 An additional early payment discount of 3% applies if invoices are
settled within 10 days.

3.4 Price escalation: Fixed pricing for the first 36 months. Thereafter,
annual increases capped at 3% or CPI, whichever is lower.

3.5 Late payment: 8% annual interest plus EUR 40 recovery costs per
late invoice (per Italian Legislative Decree 231/2002).

================================================================================
SECTION 4 - SERVICE LEVELS
================================================================================

4.1 Platform Availability: 99.95% monthly uptime.

4.2 Dedicated Financial Services Support:
    - P1 (Trading/Core Banking Impact): 15-minute response, 1-hour resolution
    - P2 (Regulatory Reporting Impact): 30-minute response, 4-hour resolution
    - P3 (General): 2-hour response, 24-hour resolution

4.3 Service Credits: 10% of monthly fee for each hour of P1 downtime
beyond the SLA, uncapped. The Bank may also claim actual damages.

================================================================================
SECTION 5 - PENALTIES
================================================================================

5.1 Regulatory Non-Compliance: If any failure of the Supplier's system
directly causes the Bank to miss a regulatory filing deadline, the
Supplier shall reimburse any fine imposed by the regulator, up to
EUR 1,000,000 per incident.

5.2 AML System Failure: If the AML module fails to flag a transaction
that should have been flagged under current regulations, and this results
in regulatory action against the Bank, the Supplier shall be liable for
the full amount of any resulting fine.

   *** THIS IS AN UNCAPPED PENALTY CLAUSE ***

5.3 Data Breach: EUR 250,000 per incident plus actual damages.

================================================================================
SECTION 6 - LIABILITY
================================================================================

6.1 General liability cap: 300% of annual contract value (EUR 2,700,000).

6.2 NO CAP applies to:
    - Breaches of data protection obligations
    - AML system failures (see Section 5.2)
    - Fraud or willful misconduct

6.3 The Supplier shall maintain:
    - Professional indemnity insurance: minimum EUR 5,000,000
    - Cyber liability insurance: minimum EUR 10,000,000

================================================================================
SECTION 7 - REGULATORY AND COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

7.1 The Supplier acknowledges that the Bank is subject to supervision by
Banca d'Italia, CONSOB, and the European Central Bank (ECB).

7.2 The Supplier shall provide full access to its systems, premises,
and documentation to the Bank's regulators upon request.

7.3 The Supplier shall comply with all outsourcing requirements under
EBA Guidelines on Outsourcing (EBA/GL/2019/02).

7.4 Annual ISAE 3402 Type II audit reports shall be provided at the
Supplier's expense.

================================================================================
SECTION 8 - TERMINATION
================================================================================

8.1 The Bank may terminate immediately upon regulatory order.

8.2 Either party may terminate for material breach with 60 days' notice.

8.3 The Bank may terminate for convenience with 180 days' notice and
payment of a termination fee equal to 50% of remaining contract value.

8.4 Upon termination, the Supplier shall provide transition assistance
for up to 12 months at standard commercial rates.

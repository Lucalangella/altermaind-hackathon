PREMESSA (RECITALS)
================================================================================

WHEREAS the Client operates a network of 12 hospitals and 45 outpatient
clinics across the Lazio region and requires advanced data analytics
capabilities for patient flow optimization and operational efficiency;

WHEREAS the Provider possesses specialized expertise in healthcare data
analytics and cloud-based platform solutions;

NOW THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

================================================================================
ART. 1 - OGGETTO DEL CONTRATTO (SUBJECT MATTER)
================================================================================

The Provider shall supply:

1.1 DataVista Cloud Platform - Healthcare Edition
    - HIPAA and GDPR compliant infrastructure
    - Data residency guaranteed within Italian borders
    - Up to 200 users
    - Annual fee: EUR 195,000.00

1.2 Patient Flow Analytics Module
    - Real-time bed management dashboards
    - Predictive admission modeling
    - Annual fee: EUR 75,000.00

1.3 Custom Development - Integration with Regional Health Information System
    - HL7 FHIR integration
    - One-time development fee: EUR 60,000.00

Total Annual Value: EUR 270,000.00
Total Contract Value (24 months): EUR 600,000.00

================================================================================
ART. 2 - DURATA (TERM)
================================================================================

2.1 This Agreement shall be effective from June 1, 2024 through
May 31, 2026 (24 months).

2.2 The Agreement may be renewed by mutual written consent for additional
12-month periods. No automatic renewal shall apply.

================================================================================
ART. 3 - CORRISPETTIVI E PAGAMENTI (PRICING AND PAYMENT)
================================================================================

3.1 Payments shall be made semi-annually in advance.

3.2 Payment terms: Net 60 days from invoice.

3.3 No discounts have been applied. Standard healthcare pricing applies.

3.4 Late payment interest: Italian Legislative Decree 231/2002 statutory
rate applies.

================================================================================
ART. 4 - LIVELLI DI SERVIZIO (SERVICE LEVELS)
================================================================================

4.1 Platform Availability: 99.7% monthly uptime guaranteed.

4.2 Given the healthcare context, the Provider guarantees:
    - Emergency support available 24/7/365
    - P1 (System Down): Response within 30 minutes, resolution within 4 hours
    - P2 (Degraded Service): Response within 2 hours, resolution within 12 hours
    - P3 (Non-Critical): Response within 8 hours, resolution within 5 business days

4.3 Service credits: 3% of monthly fee per 0.1% below uptime target,
capped at 20% of monthly fee.

================================================================================
ART. 5 - PENALI (PENALTIES)
================================================================================

5.1 Custom Development Delay: If HL7 FHIR integration is not delivered
within 120 days of contract signing, a penalty of EUR 2,000 per week of
delay shall apply, up to a maximum of EUR 20,000.

5.2 Data Availability: If patient data becomes unavailable for more than
4 consecutive hours during business hours (8:00-20:00), the Provider
shall pay a penalty of EUR 10,000 per incident.

================================================================================
ART. 6 - RESPONSABILITÀ (LIABILITY)
================================================================================

6.1 The Provider's total liability shall not exceed the total contract
value (EUR 600,000).

6.2 The Provider shall maintain professional liability insurance of at
least EUR 2,000,000.

6.3 The Provider shall indemnify the Client for any fines imposed by the
Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali arising from Provider's
processing of personal health data in breach of applicable regulations.

================================================================================
ART. 7 - PROTEZIONE DEI DATI (DATA PROTECTION)
================================================================================

7.1 The Provider acts as Data Processor under Art. 28 GDPR.

7.2 All data must be stored and processed exclusively within data centers
located in Italy.

7.3 The Provider shall notify the Client of any data breach within 24
hours of detection.

7.4 Upon termination, all Client data shall be returned or securely
destroyed within 30 days, with certification of destruction.

================================================================================
ART. 8 - RISOLUZIONE (TERMINATION)
================================================================================

8.1 Either party may terminate with 90 days' written notice for material
breach not cured within 45 days.

8.2 Termination for convenience is not permitted during the initial term.

8.3 No early termination fee, as convenience termination is excluded.

================================================================================
ART. 9 - LEGGE APPLICABILE (GOVERNING LAW)
================================================================================

Italian law applies. Jurisdiction: Court of Rome.

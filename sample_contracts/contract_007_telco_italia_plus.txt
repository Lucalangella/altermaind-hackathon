ARTICLE 4 - TERM
================================================================================

4.1 This Framework Agreement runs for 36 months from November 15, 2023
to November 14, 2026.

4.2 Individual Work Orders may extend beyond the Framework expiration
by up to 6 months to allow completion.

4.3 No automatic renewal. New Framework Agreement required.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 5 - PAYMENT
================================================================================

5.1 Platform license: Quarterly in advance, net 30 days.

5.2 Fixed-price Work Orders: Milestone-based payments per individual WO.

5.3 T&M Work Orders: Monthly in arrears based on approved timesheets.

5.4 Volume discount: 10% discount on all T&M rates if annual spend
exceeds EUR 1,000,000.00. Currently active.

5.5 Late payment interest: 4% per annum.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 6 - SERVICE LEVELS
================================================================================

6.1 Platform SLA: 99.5% uptime, standard terms.

6.2 Project delivery SLAs are defined per individual Work Order.

6.3 Standard support terms apply:
    - P1: 2 hours / 8 hours
    - P2: 4 hours / 24 hours
    - P3: 8 hours / 72 hours

================================================================================
ARTICLE 7 - PENALTIES
================================================================================

7.1 Failure to meet minimum annual commitment: The Client shall pay
the difference between actual spend and EUR 750,000 as a shortfall fee.

7.2 Project delays (fixed-price WOs): 1% of WO value per week of delay,
capped at 10% of WO value.

7.3 Quality failures: If deliverables require more than 3 rounds of
revision, the Provider shall complete remaining revisions at no cost.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 8 - LIABILITY
================================================================================

8.1 Per-WO liability cap: 100% of the individual Work Order value.

8.2 Aggregate framework liability cap: EUR 2,000,000.

8.3 Standard mutual indemnification for IP infringement.

================================================================================
ARTICLE 9 - TERMINATION
================================================================================

9.1 Framework termination: 90 days' written notice by either party.

9.2 Individual WOs may be terminated with 30 days' notice, subject to
payment for work completed.

9.3 The Client may terminate the Framework immediately if the Provider
undergoes a change of control.
